Tournament page

Rules for the tournament:

  1. There will be 2 CIVs drawned, you pick one to play. The opponent does the same. Next match, you will switch CIVs and play a second game. If you win both games you win. If each player wins one game each, the one with the fastest turn wins. If both are same, its advanced tiebreak where we count points.
  2. If you loose you are out of the tournament.
  3. This game will utilize Advanced Tiebreak and Advanced variant of CIV Arabia and Greece. However Greece will get 2 culture instead of 1 for each tech the other player doesnt know. Also Aztec only gets 1 unit instead of 2 when getting great person.
  4. You must make at least one move per day, if you cannot play one day, or go on holiday, write it in the chat. The main point is to avoid that the tournament drags out and is waiting for players to finish
  5. There will be no relics used in the game. Meaning you must redraw if relics are drawn.
  6. Tile 13 is banned, meaning you must redraw tile if this appears.
